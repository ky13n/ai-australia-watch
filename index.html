Public action and campaign asks

Core campaign focus

The campaign frame is clean-energy accountability, not anti-AI messaging. The public should be able to see what these projects demand from the electricity grid and water systems, who is influencing policy, and where enforceable safeguards are missing.

Additional clean energy

Require new data centre demand to be matched by additional renewable generation, not vague claims or existing certificates.

Diesel transparency

Publish generator numbers, diesel storage, run hours, emissions and air-quality monitoring, especially near homes and schools.

Water disclosure

Show how much water each facility uses, whether it is potable or recycled, and whether low-water claims are independently verified.

Enforceable safeguards

Turn government expectations and company sustainability claims into binding approval conditions and contract requirements.
