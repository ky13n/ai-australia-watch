AI Australia Watch

AI Australia Watch is an independent, public-access hub tracking infrastructure developments, policy and regulatory frameworks, corporate interests and active lobbying actions shaping Australia's digital future. The platform aggregates public records to map the broad social, political, and environmental landscape surrounding AI expansion across both state and federal levels. The sites purpose is to provide clear information for those looking to help shape technology governance to ensure the safe deployment of automated systems that prioritise democratic accountability, environmental sustainability, and the broader public interest.

Energy, Water & Environmental Impacts

AI and cloud infrastructure can create major electricity, water, land-use and backup-power demands. Select a data centre below to review the available energy, water, diesel backup, clean-energy and verification information for that project.

Future AI and cloud data centre proposals should be required to demonstrate additional, local and enforceable renewable energy supply, clear water-use reporting, diesel backup transparency and cumulative grid-impact assessment. Company sustainability claims are treated as claims unless they are site-specific, measurable and enforceable.
