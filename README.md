# AI Australia Watch

A public-interest dashboard tracking AI infrastructure, policy, corporate influence, and accountability issues in Australia.

## Mission

Make AI infrastructure, policy, and corporate influence in Australia visible, understandable, and actionable.

## First version

The first version will focus on AI-related data centres and infrastructure in Australia.

It will include:

- an interactive Australia map
- data centre locations and project status
- government decision-makers
- policy and legislation updates
- company profiles
- lobbying and influence information
- environmental concerns, including energy and water
- privacy, consent, and security concerns
- links to public campaigns and action pathways

## Audience

This project is for:

- the general public
- journalists
- activists
- local communities affected by AI infrastructure projects

## Positioning

AI Australia Watch is a public-interest watchdog.

It will use factual sources, plain-English summaries, risk indicators, and links to relevant public action.
